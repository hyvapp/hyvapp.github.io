Hyv FAQ's
What is Hyv?
It’s an online platform where you share content with your friends, filtered through common interests.
How can I join Hyv?
The app can be downloaded from the play store or the app store. To register, you just need your phone number. Simple.
Who can join Hyv?
You, your friends, your family, your coworkers, anyone and everyone with a smart phone. (Do note that you can only ask your phone book contacts to join your Hyvs. So dig in that contact book right away!)
What is ‘a’ Hyv?
A Hyv is an interest group on the app. Like, science or health or arts. Each is made up of the people from your phone contact book who share similar tastes.
What interests can I select?
We worked hard to slim it down to comprehensive topics to start with. And we’ll keep adding more in coming times.
Why did we create the Hyv app?
In a world of noise and half-hearted connections that social media calls friends, we realised that the digital world is making reading content we like tougher and tougher. Plus, it doesn't help when even the best and intelligent analytics engines fail in understanding what people like and want. So we decided to keep it simple, exclusive and tight: share relevant content with relevant people. This way you stay connected for content with your friends without the noise and strangers.
So how does it work?
Once you register on the app, you get to choose your interests or Hyvs. These small groups are populated with other people who have also registered on the app, but they are on your phone contact book. To share, all you need to do is choose content you like from any source. The app filters it to the relevant Hyvs.
How many interests can I sign up for?
There is no limit to the interests you can choose. Or the friends from your contact book who can join your Hyv.
How can I join a Hyv?
You could choose according to you liking from the list you are presented. You are also shown the people from your contact book who have the Hyv app. Only these people can share content with you.
How do I block someone from my Hyv?
You can choose to block or unblock anyone, at anytime. Who you choose to block, is only known to you and the other person does not see who has blocked them. Go to MY HYV and click to see the people in your contact book who have the Hyv app.
How can I create a new Hyv?
You can’t customise an interest, but really join one from the list available to you. Additionally, you can spread the word about your Hyv by sharing the app link from the ‘Post on social’ section to get friends on board. Do give us feedback if we are missing an interest group you feel strongly about.
Can strangers find me on the Hyv app?
Only people from your phone contact book can see you in a Hyv. This isn't a discovery app but one that encourages you to build better relationships with people you already know through the content flow between you. It’s a platform for likeminded people. Here no one is a stranger.
Will I get spam on the app?
We don’t allow any sales promotions on the app. Nor do we share your information with anyone. The contest is curated by you and your friends. We are only the facilitators.
How to you use Hyv to share content?
Ah, this is where things get stupidly simple. You can share from any platform with our Hyv button. The app will read the content and filter it to the appropriate Hyv. Without bothering you at all. Similarly to read the content shared by your friends you just open the app and browse through the feed. Additionally, we have options to bookmark or reshare the content on the app as well. And if you’d like to delete something you shared, just hop across to ‘Manage my Shares’ and delete.
